Stone

is a
European
folk
tale
about
hungry
travelers
who
convince
towns
people
that
they
can
make
soup
just
by
boiling
a
stone.
They
ask
people
to
add
an
ingredient
or
two
until
they
have
made
a
real
soup
for
everyone
to
share.
The
story
is
told
all
over
the
world
in
various
forms
like
nail
soup,
axe
soup,
button
soup,
and
wood
soup.

stone
soup
will
be
made
out
of
whatever
you
bring
to

State
Street
Unit 5
New
Haven
on
Thursday
Nov.

PM
